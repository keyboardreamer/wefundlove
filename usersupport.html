How to upload a fundraiser?

By clicking the create button. After clicking the create button, you can follow the instructions and fill all the information required


How to report a fundraiser?

If you see any fundraisers that violates our rules or inappropraite, please report the fundraiser through the email wefundlove@protonmail.com. We will reply you and take action in 24 hours.


How to create an account?

Firstly, you can click the account page in the navigation bar. Then, you will see an option of creating account or log in. Press create account and fill in all the information.


How to check the update of a fundraiser

First, click in to the fundraiser page. Then, scrool down and click the update button. Then, you will see all the updates on this project.
